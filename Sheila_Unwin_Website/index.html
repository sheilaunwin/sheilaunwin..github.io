About Dr. Unwin

As a leader in Learning and Development, I view education as the key to unlocking individual potential and organizational success. With a deep-rooted passion for teaching, I’ve spent almost two decades designing and implementing learning strategies beyond just skill acquisition—transforming mindsets and fostering lifelong learning. When learning is inclusive, engaging, and aligned with real-world challenges, I believe it becomes the foundation for sustainable growth.

Specializing in developing global learning solutions, I’ve guided teams in designing and delivering programs that empower employees at all levels—from emerging talent to senior leaders. My expertise lies in bridging the gap between complex organizational goals and the tailored learning experiences needed to meet them. Whether it’s leadership development, technical training, or compliance education, I create solutions that resonate with learners and drive tangible outcomes.

At the heart of my work is the continuous pursuit of innovation. I leverage emerging technologies, including AI-driven tools and e-learning platforms, to create dynamic and adaptable learning environments. By implementing data-driven strategies, I ensure that every program is impactful and measurable, focusing on outcomes that enhance employee engagement and organizational performance.

Collaboration is essential to everything I do. I have a strong track record of working with cross-functional teams, senior leadership, and external partners to build cohesive, high-performing learning ecosystems. My goal is always to create a culture of continuous improvement where learning is accessible, scalable, and aligned with the organization's strategic goals and its people's career aspirations.

More than just a learning strategist, I consider myself a teacher at heart. I strive to nurture curiosity, foster growth, and inspire others to take ownership of their learning journeys. By integrating cutting-edge educational technologies with proven learning methodologies, I help organizations meet today’s demands and prepare for tomorrow's challenges.

If your organization values a forward-thinking approach to learning, I would love to explore how we can collaborate to create impactful, learner-centered programs that drive individual and company success.
